How it works

From outsider to insider in three moves.

01

Follow the story

Every season: a new company, a new cast, a new story. For 30 days, the CEO, the CFO, rival executives, and the analysts covering them message you directly — venting, leaking, gloating, sometimes lying. You’re not reading about the story. You’re inside it.

02

Call the move

The cast doesn’t just tell you the story — they ask you where it’s going. Will earnings hit? Does the CEO hold her composure? Who’s bluffing and who’s about to break? Predictions arrive mid-conversation, posed by the same character who just filled you in. Make your call and wait for the story to prove you right.

03

Make your name

Every call you nail compounds. Every one you blow costs you. At season’s end, one leaderboard remembers who saw it coming and who got played. The money resets. The reputation doesn’t.
